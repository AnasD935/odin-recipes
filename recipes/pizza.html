Pizza

Description

it is a popular italian dish made with a flatbread crust topped with tomato sauce cheese and vegetables baked until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.

  • tomato sauce
  • olive oil
  • toppings of your choice

Steps:

  2. preheat oven
  3. add base
  4. add cheese & toppings
  5. bake
